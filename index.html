 Fast Services

We try to show no slack at all with our services. We keep your servers up at all times and when you decide to leave, your sever is archived and will be in the "Cover6 Central Server" for months just in case you decide to come back for it!

 Full Protection

By default, all servers are equipped with automatic DDoS attack mitigation in the event of an attack (reactive mitigation). You also have access to permanent mitigation (permanent rules) as well as Network Firewall configuration.

 Customizable Server

Customize your server before purchase! If you have a higher plan, you will be able to use your panel to customize your server! If you have a lower plan, your server will be managed by the "Cover6" staff members!
